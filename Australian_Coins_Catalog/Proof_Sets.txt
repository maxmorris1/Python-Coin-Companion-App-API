ANNUAL PROOF SETS
================================================================================

DESCRIPTION:
Official Royal Australian Mint (RAM) proof coin sets released annually

YEARS AVAILABLE:
1966, 1967, 1968, 1969, 1970, 1971, 1972, 1973, 1974, 1975, 1976, 1977, 1978,
1979, 1980, 1981, 1982, 1983, 1984, 1985, 1986, 1987, 1988, 1989, 1990, 1991,
1992, 1993, 1994, 1995, 1996, 1997, 1998, 1999, 2000, 2001, 2002, 2003, 2004,
2005, 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017,
2018, 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025, 2026

TOTAL SETS: 61 annual sets (1966-2026)

WHAT'S INCLUDED:
Each annual proof set contains proof versions of all circulating coins for that year:
- 1c and 2c (1966-1991 only)
- 5c, 10c, 20c, 50c (all years)
- $1 (1984 onwards)
- $2 (1988 onwards)

CHARACTERISTICS:
- Mirror-like finish on coins
- Sharper detail than circulation coins
- Presented in official RAM cases
- Include certificate of authenticity
- Limited mintage numbers

VALUE FACTORS:
- Earlier years (1966-1980s) generally more valuable
- Commemorative year sets (e.g., 1988 Bicentenary) worth more
- Complete unbroken runs highly sought after
- Condition of case and certificate affects value

MOST VALUABLE SETS:
• 1966 - First decimal proof set
• 1970 - Captain Cook commemoration
• 1988 - Bicentennial year
• 2000 - Millennium year
• 2001 - Federation year
• 2022 - Queen Elizabeth II memorial year
• 2023 - King Charles III coronation year

NOTES:
- Proof sets are sold directly by the Royal Australian Mint
- Not intended for circulation
- Popular with serious collectors
- Often sold out and increase in value over time
- Special edition proof sets released for major events
