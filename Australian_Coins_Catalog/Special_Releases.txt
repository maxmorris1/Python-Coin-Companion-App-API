SPECIAL RELEASES & COLLECTOR SETS
================================================================================

ANNUAL MINT SETS (UNCIRCULATED):
Years: 1966-2026
Description: Uncirculated coins in official RAM packaging
Contents: All circulating denominations for that year
Notes: More affordable than proof sets, coins in mint condition
       Popular gift items for births, weddings, anniversaries

BABY MINT SETS:
Years: 1966-2026
Description: Special packaging for newborns
Contents: All current circulation coins for birth year
Notes: Often given as christening/birth gifts
       Nostalgic value for recipients when they grow up

WEDDING COIN SETS:
Years: Various special releases
Description: Commemorative sets for weddings
Examples:
  • 1981 - Charles & Diana
  • 2011 - William & Catherine
  • Generic wedding sets available most years

OLYMPIC GAMES COLLECTIONS:

• 2000 - Olympic Games Sydney Series (28 coins)
  Most comprehensive Australian Olympic coin collection
  Includes $5 coins for each sport
  Highly collectible complete sets

• 2006 - Commonwealth Games Melbourne (18-coin set)
  Features different sports
  Gold-plated and standard versions available

• 2008 - Beijing Olympics Coin Collection
  Australian team commemoratives

• 2012 - London Olympics
  Team Australia series

• 2016 - Rio Olympics
  Green & gold editions

• 2020 - Tokyo Olympics (released 2021)
  Delayed release due to pandemic
  Paralympic editions also released

• 2024 - Paris Olympics
  Latest Olympic commemoratives

ALPHABET SERIES:

• 2012-2026 - A-Z 20c Alphabet Collection
  One coin for each letter of the alphabet
  Features Australian icons (A for Australia Post, etc.)
  Very popular with children and collectors
  Complete sets increasingly valuable

MINTMARK COLLECTIONS:

• 2018 - Mintmark Gold $1 Coins
  Five versions: M (Melbourne), S (Sydney), C (Canberra),
                 B (Brisbane), A (Adelaide)
  Limited release, highly sought after

• 2019 - RAM Mintmark Set
  Various denominations with different mintmarks
  Collectible packaging

• 2022 - Honey Bee Mintmark Series ($2)
  C, S, M, B editions
  Extremely popular, sold out quickly

COLLECTOR ALBUMS & FOLDERS:

• 2020-2026 - RAM Official Collector Albums
  Pre-printed folders for coin series
  $2 commemorative albums
  50c commemorative albums
  Complete decimal collection albums

SPECIAL THEMED SETS:

• 2003 - Year of the Volunteer
• 2008 - Centenary of Scouting
• 2012-2015 - Remembrance/ANZAC Series
• 2016 - 50 Years of Decimal Currency Set
• 2019 - Apollo 11 Moon Landing 50th
• 2020 - Australian Bushfire Heroes
• 2022 - Indigenous Culture Series

DISCONTINUED COIN SETS:

• Final 1c and 2c Sets (1991)
  Last year before withdrawal
  Complete with both denominations

• Changeover Sets (1966)
  Transition from pounds to dollars
  Historical significance

ROYAL EVENTS:

• Coronation Sets (1953, 2023)
• Jubilee Sets (1977, 2002, 2012, 2022)
• Royal Visit Sets (various years)
• Royal Wedding Sets (1981, 2011)

NOTES:
- Special releases often sell out quickly
- Complete sets more valuable than individual coins
- Original RAM packaging essential for value
- Commemorative year sets (2000, 2001, 2016, 2022) particularly valuable
- Olympic sets from 2000 Sydney Games are most valuable
- Alphabet series gaining popularity with new collectors
- Mintmark versions significantly more valuable than standard releases
